My Name

Hello there! I'm Shreeya Choudhary, a first-year undergraduate student pursuing B.S. Mathematics at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay). Originally from the vibrant city of Indore, Madhya Pradesh, I've found my academic home in the heart of Mumbai. Beyond the world of numbers and equations, I find joy in a symphony of diverse interests. Singing, painting, dancing, and playing multiple musical instruments are not just my hobbies; they are the colors that paint the canvas of my life. Each note, stroke, and movement allows me to express myself in unique ways. On campus, I wear multiple hats as a Class Representative and an active member of various clubs. Being part of these initiatives not only enhances my leadership skills but also allows me to contribute to the vibrant community at IIT Bombay. I thrive on connections and believe in the power of collaboration. Whether it's discussing the beauty of mathematics, sharing artistic endeavors, or exploring the rhythms of life, I am always eager to connect with like-minded individuals. You can find me on Instagram and LinkedIn, where I share my journey and engage in inspiring conversations. Thank you for taking a glimpse into my world. I look forward to connecting with you and sharing more about my experiences, passions, and the exciting journey of exploration that college life brings. Feel free to reach out, and let's create some meaningful connections together!
